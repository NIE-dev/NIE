About


The National Innovation Ecosystem (NIES) is an online platform where all kinds of support inventors need for the commercialization process are made available with ease of access. NIES provides a mentor to each inventor, and brings services and resources to the inventor according to his/her needs, throughout the innovation journey. At NIES thereâ€™s always someone to get advice from, and resources accessible quite conveniently - a dream come true for inventors. NIES comprises seven support networks as mentoring, equipment/services, skills, IP management, investment, legal, and business development. The NIES has been developed by the NIA, NSF, LATTO, ICTA, SLIC, NIPO and ICTA
